I'm Jen. I like it when things just work.

As a UX designer I find great satisfaction in solving problems for the user. I enjoy working to understand what a user needs and wants, then turning that into something concrete. I believe the best designs are ones people don't really think about, but are used without frustration and spark a bit of joy.

I have a background in environmental engineering that gave me an understanding of how to tackle complex problems and work with teammates from a wide array of disciplines.

I have always liked to make things, whether that is knitting a hat or designing a website. This love naturally carries over to my UX design. I'm excited to create new user centered solutions. Let's chat!
